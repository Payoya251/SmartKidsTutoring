Frequently Asked Questions

Is tutoring free?

Yes! All services are 100% free for students K–12.

How do I sign up?

Click on "Sign Up" and fill in your info. We'll follow up via email.

Are the tutors trained?

Yes! All tutors complete a short training and background check.

How long are the sessions?

Each session is typically 30–45 minutes, depending on the subject.

What subjects do you offer tutoring in?

We offer tutoring in a wide range of subjects, including Math, Science, English, and Social Studies. Please see the "Services" page for a more detailed list.

What are the qualifications of your tutors?

Our tutors are volunteers and often students themselves who have strong academic backgrounds in the subjects they tutor. They are passionate about helping others learn and undergo a basic vetting process.

How do I connect with a tutor?

Once you sign up, you'll be able to browse available tutors and their schedules through your student dashboard. You can then book a session that works for you.

What if I have technical issues during a session?

If you experience technical difficulties, please try refreshing your browser or checking your internet connection. If the problem persists, contact our support team through the "Contact Us" page.

Can I request a specific tutor?

Yes, if a particular tutor's schedule and availability match your needs, you can book sessions with them directly through the platform.

What if I need to cancel a session?

Please refer to our cancellation policy outlined in your student dashboard. Generally, we request that you cancel with at least 24 hours' notice.
