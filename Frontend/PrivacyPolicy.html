Privacy Policy

At SharpKidsTutoring, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. What We Collect

We may collect your name, email, account info, usage data, and more to provide our services.

2. How We Use It

To manage accounts, pair students with tutors, process payments, and provide support.

3. Children's Privacy

We only collect student data with verified parent/guardian consent and use it for educational purposes only.

4. Sharing

We never sell your data. We may share info with service providers as needed to run the site.

5. Cookies

We may use cookies to personalize the experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You can request to update or delete your account at any time.

7. Contact

Questions? Contact us anytime.
