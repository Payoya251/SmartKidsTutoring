About Sharp Kids Tutoring

Built by students. For students. Powered by purpose.

How We Started

Sharp Kids Tutoring began as a college class project. Our team saw an opportunity to make a real impact by offering free, fun, and flexible tutoring to K–12 students — powered by students and for students.

Our Mission

We believe every child deserves personalized, engaging education. Our mission is to connect them with tutors who genuinely care, creating opportunities for confidence and curiosity to grow.

Our Values

  • Personalized learning
  • Growth mindset
  • Kindness & empathy
  • Community-driven impact
